Our story

A note from us

Our story actually started all the way back in 2015.

We sat in the exact same high school classroom, completely unaware of each other. Fast forward to 2020, when a simple swipe on Bumble changed everything. It felt as though fate finally took our hands and brought us together at exactly the right time. From that first conversation, we were completely inseparable.

Michael is my absolute rock, the ultimate ‘yes, can do’ kind of man. He takes care of me in his own gentle, quiet way, bringing a peace to my life that feels like a calm, soothing lake. No matter how wild or crazy my ideas are, he never doubts them. He just looks at me, smiles, and says, ‘Yes, let’s do it!’ That unwavering love and support is exactly my why.

We are so incredibly excited to step into this next chapter of our lives, and we can’t wait to celebrate our love story with all of our favorite people.

Thank you for being a part of our journey. Can’t wait to see you all there!

Getting there

Parking Information

Free parking is available directly on-site at Sunnybank Plaza. The best place to park is the multi-story car park, or the open-air parking lot closest to the Landmark Restaurant entrance.

02

Attire

Dress Code

Dress to feel your best! We recommend Semi-Formal or Smart Formal attire. We can’t wait to see everyone looking amazing.
